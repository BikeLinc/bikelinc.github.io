I have experience in various programming languages, including:

  • Python

  • AVR Assembly

Additionally, I am proficient in several electronic design automation (EDA) tools, such as Autodesk Eagle, LT Spice, and Electric VLSI, and experience with Cadence Design Suite.

I also have experience working with microcontrollers for environmental air-quality applications. On top of that, I have hands-on expertise in nano-fabrication and I just wrapped up two-semesters of overseeing photolithography processes for our senior project, where successfully built functional PMOS MOSFETs.
