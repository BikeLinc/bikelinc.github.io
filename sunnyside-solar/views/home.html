Sunnyside SolarSim

A Visualization and Simulation Tool for the Sunnyside Solar Community Solar Farm in Durango, CO.

This tool is powered by LPEA and the Fort Lewis Virtual Solar Lab.

This web application uses PVLib for modeling photovoltaic systems.

Getting Started
Step 1: Run a Simulation to generate results.
Step 2: View your results in the Explore Data tab.
Step 3: Download your results as a CSV file from the Metrics & Export tab.
